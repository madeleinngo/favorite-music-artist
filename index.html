Local Sound

"Local Sound" is a movement born from within the Nashville-based college ministry, MyLocal. Their identity is found in the hope of Jesus Christ and as key holders to the kingdom of heaven, doing whatever it takes to bring people to Jesus by glorifying God with all that they have.
