Local Sound

"Local Sound" is a movement born from within the Nashville-based college ministry, MyLocal. Their identity is found in the hope of Jesus Christ and as key holders to the kingdom of heaven, doing whatever it takes to bring people to Jesus by glorifying God with all that they have.

Lyrics

Nothing comes between us I will be at peace Because I know the Shepherd Cause He is good to me

I like these lyrics from their song "Shepherd" because it is based on Psalm 23 from the Bible, a psalm that is personally a source of strength for me.

I also like that Local Sound wrote it like this, from the point of view of the sheep because it helps with the feel of the song, which is inspirational and moving.
